01 / Architecture Declaration

AI-Native Architecture Declaration

This is not about adding AI to existing systems (AI-wrapped). This is about redefining business operations from first principles, with Agents as the core. Every business process is inherently Agent-driven. Every interaction is inherently completed through natural language. Every decision is inherently supported by LLM reasoning. This is not a transformation — this is a rebirth.
